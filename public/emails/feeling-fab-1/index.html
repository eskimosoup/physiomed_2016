Feeling Fab Exercises: Do you ever suffer from a sore neck?
We know that certain repetitive activities in the workplace such as; sitting or standing for long periods of time; lifting objects or operating machine tools contribute to this. These activities, if not addressed, lead to the gradual build-up of tension and stiffness in the neck, upper back and shoulder areas.

Our team of Chartered Physiotherapists have designed these exercises to assist in preventing the onset of tension and stiffness or to ease any pain if it occurs.
