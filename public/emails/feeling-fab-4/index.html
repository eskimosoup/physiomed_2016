Feeling Fab Exercises: Do you ever suffer from shoulder pain?
We know that certain repetitive activities in the workplace, such as lifting or carrying for long periods of time or operating machine tools can contribute to this. These activities, if not addressed, lead to the gradual build-up of tension and stiffness in the shoulders.

Our team of Chartered Physiotherapists have designed these exercises to assist in preventing the onset of tension and stiffness or to ease any pain if it occurs.

About Award Winning Physio Med
We provide physiotherapy solutions for workforces across the UK. Where physiotherapy can help, we get your team back to work more quickly, improving their health and wellbeing, whilst increasing their productivity.
We won the 2012 Chartered Society of Physiotherapy "promoting self-management" clinical excellence award, for our Physiotherapy Advice Line (PAL) service. We provide a blended approach to physiotherapy treatment which includes:
  • Telephone support
  • Online videos
  • Hands-on Physiotherapy
On average, 65% of patients diagnosed within 12 weeks can self-manage their condition with only 100 minutes of remote support and no requirement for face-to-face appointments.

Using our PAL service can save your organisation up to 35% compared to traditional physiotherapy solutions, whilst maintaining your clinical outcomes.
